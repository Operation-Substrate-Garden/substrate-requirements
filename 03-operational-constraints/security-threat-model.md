# Security and Threat Modeling

Operation Substrate Garden's security must be uncompromising.

## 1. Zero-Trust Architecture
- **Verification:** All users and systems must be verified at every access point.
- **Micro-Segmentation:** Access to each community and topic is isolated from other parts of the substrate.
- **Identity:** All actions are tied to a verified identity and organization.

## 2. Threat Modeling
- **Data Breaches:** Prevention through encryption at rest and in transit.
- **Unauthorized Access:** Defense through whitelisting and enterprise-grade authentication.
- **Insider Threats:** Monitoring and auditing of user activity within sensitive communities.
- **Denial of Service:** Infrastructure protection to ensure high availability.

## 3. Compliance and Data Integrity
- **Auditing:** Regular, automated security audits are performed across the entire substrate.
- **Data Governance:** Data remains within specified geographic regions as required by whitelisted organizations.
- **Provenance:** The platform tracks the origin of all content to ensure authority and prevent misinformation.
