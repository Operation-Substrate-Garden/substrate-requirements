# Substrate Map

The Substrate Map is the primary visual navigation system for Operation Substrate Garden.

## 1. Visual Navigation
- **Definition:** A two-dimensional, interactive map of the entire platform's communities and topics.
- **Dynamic Representation:** Communities are represented as nodes, with their size and proximity determined by activity and shared interests.
- **Filtering:** Users can filter the map by community category, organization, or personal membership.
- **Search Integration:** When a user searches for a topic or community, the map highlights relevant results and their place in the substrate.

## 2. Tactical Elegance Design
- **Tonal Layering:** The map uses subtle gradients and tonal changes to distinguish between different layers of information.
- **No Borders:** Nodes and paths are defined by color and opacity rather than 1px borders.
- **Smooth Interaction:** Zooming and panning must be fluid and performant, providing a sense of "air" and space.

## 3. Organizational Context
- **Whitelisted Regions:** The map clearly delineates areas of the substrate that are exclusive to certain whitelisted organizations.
- **Shared Space:** Neutral areas of the map represent communities open to all verified users from any whitelisted organization.
